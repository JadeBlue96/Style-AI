About the project

Style AI is a web service based on the Style Transfer Generative model created by Leon A. Gatys, Alexander S. Ecker, and Matthias Bethge and documented in their paper A Neural Algorithm of Artistic Style in 2015.
To make this model work, famous deep learning architectures for image recognition are utilized such as VGG16 and VGG19.
